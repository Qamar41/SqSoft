We make good Engineers. They, in turn, make good software.

SQSoft began as a one-man company, by the founder Qamar Ashfaq . We have been working in multiple development technologies since our inception in 2019; staying at par with the latest trends and demands of the industry. Besides being trusted by some multinational business giants, most of our work has been for Entrepreneurs passionate to make their mark in the world. One such highflier is International Checkout, which started in a garage, but is now recognized as an Inc. 500 Company in the U.S. And guess what? We are still working for them.

At the moment, we have grown to 40+ Professionals working in various Software Wings in 3 major cities of Pakistan: Lahore, Islamabad and Multan. Our Developers are picked after a fine recruitment process in which their excellence and ability to excel is measured.

To keep up-to-minute with the latest I.T. trends, we have a dedicated Training Department to help Developers familiarize themselves with new technologies and frameworks. Right now, we have Pakistan’s finest software breed working in PHP, .NET (Web and Desktop), Ruby on Rails, Python and Mobile projects. Now a days we are doing advance projects of Machine Learning , Deep Learning , Computer Vision and Data Sciene

Due to an intelligent strategy and continuous hard work by our team, SQSoft is being counted amongst Pakistan’s largest software houses which are growing in a very less time. And keeping with the current pace, we are on our way to be the Largest.
